Our Story

BORN IN
PAISLEY.
RAISED ON PIES.

Owner Scott McGinlay grew up with food in his blood — his father ran a butcher's shop right here in Paisley. After working his way through the deli trade from the age of 25, Scott opened Paisley Pie Co. on Bridge Street in spring 2023, before moving the business to its bigger flagship home on Causeyside Street.

The concept was simple: if it tastes amazing, it can go in a pie. If Scott was cooking for himself and thought "that would be good in a pie," it went straight on the menu. Customers can even suggest their own flavour combinations in the request book — and if the pie makes the cut, it gets named after them.

"Any time I'm cooking or making things for myself, I found myself thinking — that would be good in a pie." — Scott McGinlay, Founder

Within months the shop outgrew Bridge Street entirely and the main store moved to Causeyside Street. A motion was tabled in the Scottish Parliament to congratulate the business. BBC News covered the story. Paisley Pie Co. now supplies Key Stores and Nisa nationally — with a Glasgow expansion planned.
